Neural Network Data Model

Temperatures grow significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Latitude), with relatively steady temperatures for cities located 20 degrees above and below the equator.

However, cities below the equator showed higher temperatures due to it being the start of summer below the equator versus the start of winter above the equator.

This is reflected in the greatest concentration of cities with weather data, which are located between 20 degrees and 60 degrees above the equator. Those temperatures ranged from the mid-80s F to 20-degrees F.
